Sheet Pan Parmesan Chicken and Veggies

This extra-crispy sheet pan chicken and veggies dinner is baked on one pan, packed with flavor, and makes for quick cleanup!

Ingredients

    1 tbsp dried oregano
    1 tbsp dried parsley
    1 tsp paprika
    1/2 tsp garlic powder
    1/4 tsp seasoned salt
    1/4 tsp ground black pepper
    1/2 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
    1 small red potato
    1 sweet bell pepper
    1 cup broccoli florets
    1 tbsp minced garlic
    3 tbsp olive oil
    1/3 cup all-purpose flower
    4 tbsp butter, melted
    1 cup panko bread crumbs
    1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
    1 1/2 lb skinless, boneless chicken breast

Cooking Instructions

    Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
    Combine oregano, parsley, paprika, garlic powder, seasoned salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
    Place green beans, potato, bell pepper, broccoli, and garlic onto sheet pan. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle 1/2 of the seasoning mix; toss to coat.
    Place flour into a bowl. Place melted butter into a second bowl. Mix panko, 2/3 cup Parmesan cheese, and the reaming seasoning mix together in a third bowl.
    Slice chicken in 1 1/4-inch strips, then coat in flour. Dredge floured strips in melted butter, dripping excess butter back into bowl. Press into the Parmesan-panko mixture until heavily coated on both sides.
    push veggies to one half of sheet pan. Place coated chicken onto the other side. Sprinkle any remaining Parmesan-panko mixture over the chicken, pressing to adhere.
    Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes. Flip chicken and stir veggies, then continue baking until chicken juices run clear and veggies are crisp-tender 10-15 more minutes.
    Meanwhile, make the dipping sauce: Whisk mayo, ketchup, garlic powder, and Worcestershire sauce together in a small bowl.
    Remove chicken and veggies from oven. Toss veggies with remaining 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese. Serve with dipping sauce.
